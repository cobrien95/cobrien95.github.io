Impact & Support

Advancing care, education, and health for orphaned and vulnerable children on Mfangano Island through community-rooted programs and transparent partnerships.

How to Support
Current ReachSupporting 50 orphaned and vulnerable children with established programming including education, healthcare, and nutrition.

Cultural Preservation

Maintaining children's connection to Suba and Luo heritage while providing modern education and opportunities.

Educational Achievement

All eligible children enrolled in formal schooling with full support for school fees, uniforms, supplies, and educational materials.
Community EmpowermentTraining local youth volunteers in childcare, mentorship, and leadership to build sustainable local capacity.
Long-term OutcomesPreparing children to become self-reliant community members through vocational training and life skills development.
Community PresenceOperating as a registered CBO in Mfangano Island, serving Suba and Luo speaking communities.
