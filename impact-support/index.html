Impact & Support

Advancing care, education, and health for orphaned and vulnerable children on Mfangano Island through community-rooted programs and transparent partnerships.

How to Support

Current Reach

Supporting 50 orphaned and vulnerable children with established programming including education, healthcare, and nutrition.

Cultural Preservation

Maintaining children's connection to Suba and Luo heritage while providing modern education and opportunities.

Educational Achievement

All eligible children enrolled in formal schooling with full support for school fees, uniforms, supplies, and educational materials.
Community EmpowermentTraining local youth volunteers in childcare, mentorship, and leadership to build sustainable local capacity.
Long-term OutcomesPreparing children to become self-reliant community members through vocational training and life skills development.
Community PresenceOperating as a registered CBO in Mfangano Island, serving Suba and Luo speaking communities.

How to Support

Financial Contributions

  • Annual operating budget of 1,783,000 KES ($10,000 USD) with transparent line-item breakdown available.
  • Monthly Child Sponsorship - $200 (complete care package)
  • Emergency Fund contributions
  • Specific Item Sponsorship:
  • School uniforms: $30 per child
  • Medical care: $12 per child annually
  • Monthly food: $60 per child
  • Educational materials: $6 per child

Partnership Opportunities

Seeking collaborations with NGOs, government agencies, and international donors for facility development:

  • Corporate program sponsorship
  • Professional volunteer opportunities
  • Visit arrangements to Mfangano Island
  • Employee engagement experiences
